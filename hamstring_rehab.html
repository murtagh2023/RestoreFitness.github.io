Hamstring Rehabilitation Program

Rebuild strength, mobility, and function

Program Structure

Day-based format focusing on mobility, strength, and return to sport. Move to the next day when goals are achieved, not on a fixed timeline.

Program Notes, Tips, & Suggested Layouts

  • Do every exercise on both sides unless specified.
  • br = breath, e/s = each side, sec = seconds.
  • Rounds: One round of exercises completed back-to-back without rest in between. Rest is taken at the end.
  • Work at a level where you feel challenged, but not overwhelmed.
  • This program is about learning and gradual progress.

Example Layout

  • Monday: Day 1 - Breathing, Mobility & Strength
  • Tuesday: Recovery/Mobility Day (optional)
  • Wednesday: Day 2 - Breathing, Mobility & Strength
  • Thursday: Recovery/Mobility Day (optional)
  • Friday: Day 3 - Breathing, Mobility & Strength
  • Saturday: Rest Day
